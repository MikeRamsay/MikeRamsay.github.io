Reusables at Races Program Activation Project

2025 Intern Project with the City of Seattle

January 2025 - July 2025

Project Summary

This was the main project I worked on during my internship with the City of Seattle. This was essentially an extension of the Reuse Seattle Program, which I helped launch during the summer of 2024. The main emphasis of this program is to incentivise local businesses, event organizers and organizations to shift away from using single use food and dishware projects and to reusables instead. I was responsible for identifying a sector which could benefit from the implementation of reusables. I then conducted some research and identified a company which makes reusable silicone cups, designed for run and walk events. I got in contact with the business owner and we were able to purchase 5,000 cups from them to use in Seattleâ€™s own Reuse Program. This first race involved a partnership with Run for Good and Friends of the Waterfront to activate the use of reusable silicone cups at their Waterfront 5k on May 31st. For this race 1,315 cups were used, significantly reducing single-use waste generated by the event. The cups were then cleaned and used at future races around the city.
