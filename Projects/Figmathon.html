RESUME

App for Food Waste Reduction

Produced during Design-a-thon with UX@UW Club

May 2022

Project Summary

I created this prototype app for a design-a-thon event with the UX @ UW Club. My team and I used Figma over the course of the two-day design-a-thon. The app allows users to track the perishable items in their fridge or pantry and donate any unwanted or excess items to help minimize food waste. The basic functionality of the app consists of the ability to scan and manually add items to the app’s “digital pantry”. Once the expiration date is imputed for an item, the app keeps track of it and notifies the user when it reaches its expiration date. Once an item has reached its listed expiration date, the user can either keep it or donate it to local food banks as they still accept many items past their “best before” dates. The app also has a grocery list and food planning functions to reduce the amount of excess food the user is buying and create meals based on which ingredients are available. My team ended up being one of the finalists and presented our project to a panel of judges at the end of the competition.
