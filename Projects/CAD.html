Computer Aided Design Project

Final project produced to showcase skills gained from ME 123: Intro to CAD at UW

May 2025 - June 2025

Project Summary

I completed this project when I took the intro to computer-aided design class in the mechanical engineering department at the University of Washington. Throughout the quarter I learned how to use Solidworks, a CAD software. This was the final project, designed to showcase the skills that I had learned throughout the quarter. I decided to design, build and assemble the pipe frame kart from Mario Kart. For this project, I utilized a series of reference planes, shells, feature patterns, mechanical mates. The most difficult part was designing the frame which uses a series of sweeps to achieve the stacked pipe element. To the right is the assembly file which showcases the various pieces that I designed and mated together to build the final kart.
